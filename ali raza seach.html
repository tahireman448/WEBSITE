Early Life and Education

Ali Raza was born on January 24, 1990, in Karachi, Pakistan. He grew up in a Punjabi Muslim family in Lahore. His father, Zulfiqar Raza, is a fashion designer, and his mother is a housewife. Ali pursued a diploma in graphic design at Forman Christian College and later earned a degree in arts and design from the University of the Punjab.

Career Beginnings
Ali Raza began his career as a model in 2018, quickly gaining recognition for his work in various modeling campaigns. He made his acting debut in 2020 with the drama "Bhool Jaa Ay Dil," where he played the role of Hadid. His performance earned him widespread acclaim and marked the beginning of his transition from modeling to acting.
