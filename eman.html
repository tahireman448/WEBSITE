Early Life and Education

Ali Raza was born on January 24, 1990, in Karachi, Pakistan. He grew up in a Punjabi Muslim family in Lahore. His father, Zulfiqar Raza, is a fashion designer, and his mother is a housewife. Ali pursued a diploma in graphic design at Forman Christian College and later earned a degree in arts and design from the University of the Punjab.

Career Beginnings
Ali Raza began his career as a model in 2018, quickly gaining recognition for his work in various modeling campaigns. His performance earned him widespread acclaim and marked the beginning of his transition from modeling to acting.
